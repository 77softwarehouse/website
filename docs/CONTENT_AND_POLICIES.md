## Booking Policy

- Exact rental length: 3 months.
- Renters can start any day if the resulting 3-month period is available.
- The system calculates checkout exactly 3 months after the selected start date.
- Request-to-book approval is required.
- Manager can add blocked dates, maintenance dates, blackout windows, and minimum notice settings.
- Reservation is not final until approval, required payment/deposit, and lease signature are complete.

## Payment and Lease Policy

- Use Stripe for deposit/payment collection.
- Use a British Columbia 3-month rental workflow reviewed before launch.
- Use the official BC residential tenancy rental agreement form as the lease basis.
- Track lease status in the backend.
- Final deposit amounts, rent schedule, fees, taxes, and cancellation terms must be reviewed before production launch.
